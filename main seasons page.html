Spring

Hanami (Cherry blossom viewing) is an extremely popular event at this time of the year. A special weather forecast is shown on the news, showing which areas are in bloom and if it is perfect picnic weather.

Summer

Summer in Japan can be incredibly hot and humid. Shaved ice is a popular food to eat during this season. There are various firework displays during the summer festivals. Many people wear summer kimono called "Yukata" during the festivals.

Winter

Winter is the best time to visit if you want to avoid crowds and queues. Popular places to visit are the onsens where the snow monkeys bathe, or Hokkaido to try out some winter sports. Mt Fuji is also at its most visible.

Autumn

Autumn time in Japan is a beautiful showcase of reds and yellows, as the weather turns milder. Prices at top tourist destinatins are at their lowest during this time of the year. Temples are popular places to visit at this time of the year.
